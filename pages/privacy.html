Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

TEF Master ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) and other applicable regulations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our educational services
  • Track your learning progress across devices
  • Generate personalized AI-powered diagnostic analyses
  • Send you diagnostic results via email
  • Verify your premium access
  • Communicate important service updates

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate TEF Master:

  • Stripe — Payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy
  • Supabase — Database and authentication. Data stored on servers in the United States.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — Generating diagnostic analysis. Your test answers are sent to Claude's API for analysis. Anthropic's privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy
  • Resend — Email delivery for diagnostic results.
  • Netlify — Website hosting.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (cloud database) with Row Level Security (RLS) policies enforced. All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption. We use service-role keys for server-side operations and never expose database credentials to client-side code.

Learning progress is also stored locally in your browser (localStorage) for instant access. This local data syncs with our cloud database when you are logged in.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Diagnostic results are stored indefinitely to enable progress tracking over time. You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

6. Cookies and Local Storage

TEF Master uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to store your learning progress and login state. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

7. Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) and applicable regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data
  • Data portability — Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent — Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at cajagdishlade@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You can also reset your local progress data at any time from the Progress page using the "Reset All Progress" button.

8. Children's Privacy

TEF Master is designed for adults preparing for TEF Canada, primarily for Canadian immigration purposes. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in the United States (Supabase, Netlify, Anthropic) and other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. By using TEF Master, you consent to the transfer of your information to these jurisdictions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
